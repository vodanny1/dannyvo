about me


I am 23 years old and have a passion for technology. Growing up during the time that technology was advancing rapidly, I got to experience the good and the bad it had to offer. I did not get my first computer until around the year 2005-2006. The first computer I used was an old eMachine running on Windows 98 with a notably heavy CRT monitor. I did not get to experience the internet until a couple years after that. From then on, my interest in technology developed. I started looking into tinkering game consoles such as the PSP to play downloaded games. Most notably by that time, YouTube was becoming popular which opened a new door for my interest. I started keeping myself up to date in the world of technology through Youtube. Fast forward to highschool, I found that I was becoming interested in computer programming but didn't know where to start. It wasn't until my last year of highschool that I finally took the first step into taking a class about it and set off from there.

I graduated from the University of Toronto in June 2020 studying Statistics and Computer Science. During my university career and through my courses, I dabbled in python, java, and C to learn the fundamentals of computer science. Upon graduation, I took the course 100 Days of Swift by Hacking with Swift to learn more about iOS development. I am currently working at Yardi which is a property management software company that hosted through the web which peaked my interest in web development. I am currently learning about HTML, CSS, Javascript and learning the essential tools such as node, express, databases, and more.
